Welocome!

This is Salesforce log visualier. You can generate UML sequence from your salesforce logs.This application don't store any information.This application just works on your browser by javascript.

Usage
  • Upload salesforce debug log file at left menu
  • Click Submit button
  • After Waite a few minutes, you can get sequence diagram
  • optionaly you can filter by methods
